# 📊 Retail Sales Performance Analysis

This project provides a comprehensive data analysis and visualization of retail sales performance. It demonstrates a full data pipeline: from extracting and cleaning data using **SQL** to building a dynamic and interactive **Business Dashboard in Excel**.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview
The goal of this project is to analyze sales, profit, and quantity metrics across different categories and regions to identify business growth opportunities and operational inefficiencies.

### 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **SQL:** Data extraction, cleaning, and aggregation.
- **Microsoft Excel:** Data transformation, Pivot Tables, and Dashboard design.
- **Data Analytics:** Trend analysis, regional benchmarking, and profitability insights.

## 🔍 Key Insights (Executive Summary)
After analyzing the dataset, the following business insights were discovered:

1. **High-Margin Segments:** While **Technology** and **Office Supplies** represent roughly half of the sales volume, they contribute the vast majority of the company's total profit.
2. **The Furniture Dilemma:** The **Furniture** category has a high sales volume but significantly lower profit margins due to high operational costs and discount rates.
3. **Regional Leaders:** The **West Region** is the top-performing territory in both revenue and profitability.
4. **Efficiency Gap:** The **Central Region** shows high sales activity but lags in profit compared to the East and West, suggesting a need for supply chain optimization.
